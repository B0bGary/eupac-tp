EUPAC-TP Seminar Series

This is the webpage for the European and Pacific Theoretical Physics Seminar series.

European and Pacific Theoretical Physics is a group that runs online seminars and talks across broad topics in mathematical and theoretical physics. Our goal is to share concepts across continents to bring about a deeper understanding of cutting-edge topics in physics.

Our current focus for latter half of 2025 is Generalised (Categorical) Symmetries.
